This curriculum is designed to take a learner from **operational support and basic technical skills** to **AI-powered automation, workflow design, and applied coding**, with a strong focus on real-world business use cases.

It combines **AI tools, no-code automation, and foundational coding** to create professionals who can save time, reduce manual work, and build intelligent systems for modern organizations.
